# Business Continuity Plan (BCP)

**Practice:** Uptown Surgery
**Version:** 3.0
**Date:** Mar 2026
**Responsible Person:** Director

## 1. Introduction
This plan sets out the procedures to ensure the practice can continue to provide essential services in the event of a disruption (e.g., power failure, IT failure, flooding, pandemic).

## 3. Critical Functions (Priority Order)
1. **Emergency Access:** Divert phones to mobile/buddy practice immediately.
2. **Prescriptions:** Switch to handwritten scripts (pad stored in safe) if EPS down.
3. **Appointments:** Triage only. Cancel routine checks.

## 4. Scenario Plans

### 4.1 Loss of IT / Clinical System
- Switch to "Paper Protocol" (Box A in Manager's Office).
- Use 4G backup dongle for internet access if broadband only failed.
- Record all notes on paper proformas; scan back later.

### 4.2 Loss of Utilities (Power/Water)
- **Power:** Vaccines to be moved to "Cold Box" and transferred to 'Westside Health' (Buddy Practice) within 2 hours.
- **Water:** Sanitisers to be used for hand hygiene. Bottled water for drinking. If >4 hours, close practice.

### 4.3 loss of Premises (Fire/Flood)
- relocate critical staff to 'Westside Health'.
- Divert phone lines.
- Notify ICB and CQC.
