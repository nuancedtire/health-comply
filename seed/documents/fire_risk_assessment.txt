## 1. Executive Summary
The overall fire risk rating for the premises is **LOW/MEDIUM**. Several good practices were observed, but two key actions are required to maintain compliance.

## 2. Fire Hazards
- **Electrical Sources:** All PAT testing is up to date (sticker date: Aug 2025). Server room temp is monitored.
- **Smoking:** Strict "No Smoking" policy on site. Signage is visible.
- **Arson:** Waste bins are stored in a locked compound away from the building.

## 3. Fire Protection Measures

- **Extinguishers:** Annual service completed Sept 2025. All seals intact.
- **Emergency Lighting:** Monthly flick tests recorded by Practice Manager. Annual discharge test passed Aug 2025.

## 4. Means of Escape

- "Fire Exit" signage is compliant with BS 5499.
- **Observation:** The rear fire exit door was slightly sticking.
