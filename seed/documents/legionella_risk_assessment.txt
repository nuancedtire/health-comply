# Water Safety & Legionella Risk Assessment

**Site:** Uptown Surgery
**Assessment Date:** October 5, 2025
**Review Date:** October 2027
**Assessor:** HydroSafe Services

## 1. System Description
- **Type:** Mains fed cold water system. Instantaneous electric hot water heaters (point of use).
- **Storage:** No cold water storage tanks (CWST) on site. Direct mains feed.
- **Outlets:** 8 WHBs (Wash Hand Basins), 1 Kitchen Sink, 2 Showers (Staff).

## 2. Risk Rating
**Overall Risk: LOW**
Rationale: Simple water system, high turnover of water, no storage tanks.

## 3. Temperature Control Checks (Sentinel Points)
| Outlet | Type | Standard | Measured Temp | Result |
|--------|------|----------|---------------|--------|
| Room 1 (Nurse) | Hot Tap | >50°C (1 min) | 55.2°C | Pass |
| Room 1 (Nurse) | Cold Tap | <20°C (2 mins) | 18.5°C | Pass |
| Kitchen | Hot Tap | >50°C | 58.0°C | Pass |
| Staff Shower | Mixed | Thermostatic | 41°C | N/A (TMV Fitted) |

*Note:* All TMVs (Thermostatic Mixing Valves) serviced and failsafe tested during visit.

## 4. Recommendations
1. **Shower Usage:** Staff shower is infrequently used. **Action:** Implement weekly flushing regime for the shower to prevent stagnation.
2. **Dead Legs:** No dead legs identified.
3. **Monitoring:** Continue monthly temperature checks of sentinel taps.
