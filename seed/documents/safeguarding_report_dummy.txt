## 4. Incident Review & Learning (Anonymised)

### Case A (Child) - June 2025
**Incident:** Delay in referral for a child presenting with unexplained bruising.
**Outcome:** Referral made 48 hours late. Child safe, but process failure identified.
**Learning:** "Was Not Brought" code was not added to notes.
**Action Taken:** "Was Not Brought" policy reinforced. All DNAs for children now automatically flagged to Lead GP.

### Case B (Adult) - September 2025
**Incident:** Financial abuse suspected in elderly patient with dementia.
**Action:** GP raised concern immediately with Adult Social Care.
**Outcome:** Patient now has an allocated social worker. Positive outcome.
**Reflection:** Good example of "Professional Curiosity" by Dr. Sarah Smith.
