# Southside Medical Practice: Medication Management Policy 2024

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to ensure the safe and effective management of medicines within our practice, minimizing risks to patients and staff.

## 2. Storage
- All controlled drugs (CDs) must be stored in the designated CD cabinet, which is double-locked.
- Vaccines and other temperature-sensitive medicines must be stored in the medical refrigerator.
- Temperature logs for the medical fridge must be recorded twice daily (morning and evening).
- Any temperature excursion (outside 2°C to 8°C) must be reported immediately to the Practice Manager.

## 3. Prescribing
- All prescriptions must be issued by a qualified prescriber.
- Repeat prescriptions must be reviewed every 6 months for chronic conditions.
- We use the electronic prescription service (EPS) as the primary method for issuing prescriptions.

## 4. Disposal
- Expired or returned medicines must be placed in the designated "DOOP" (Dispose of Old Pharmaceuticals) bins.
- Bins are collected monthly by our authorized waste contractor.

## 5. Audit
- A quarterly audit of medication storage and prescribing patterns is conducted by the Clinical Lead.
- Results are discussed in the Clinical Governance meeting.
